It was quite a silent night, my parents had decided to go out to a friends place, my grandfather was at home with me and he was sleeping. i had decided to get off the phone (it was 11:00 and i felt like watching t.v) so i did, said my byes, enough talking for the night my ear was red because of the phone! so i decided to watch tv instead. i had gone to my parents bedroom to lie down in their spacious bed and switched on the tv. So i was watching tv and switching between the comedy network and to the higher channels. Then after, i decided to start flipping channels from channel 2 higher and came across gospel, and i started to question the existence of god, how could i ever? i always have, but being tired your mind decides to wander off and question things you respect and regularely think about, so i started telling myself where is the proof in god, jesus, mother mary? then all of a sudden, atop my parents shelf 2 metres above me the picture of mother mary started to rattle from the back (it was resting against the wall!) and fell down on the ground.
