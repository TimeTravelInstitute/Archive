This took place in St. Augustine, Florida in 1956. My 12 year old Mom, Toni Lee, was hanging out the clothes with my Grandmother. Three disks hovered eye-level then slowly hovered across a dirt road to where an empty field and some woods were. They were a little larger than dinner plates, silver, and silent. That night and for 6months they heard banging and clanging. Grandmaw told the girls(Toni and BF Terry) to stay out of those woods and field. The girls snuck on over to the edge of woods anyway. They could hear 4 different people talking in a clipped tongue like Chinese. Like a man and women and 2 children. My Mom turned to go and Terry just froze with fright looking up into the trees. My Mom said she actually turned a shade of white with fear. Terry turned and bolted with my Mom following suit. Not daring to look. Terry said there was a 3ft face of a monster floating in the trees. Whatever it was it was used to keep those girls out of those woods. A few days later Grandmaw was sitting on the porch at sundown when out of the woods a man in a silver jump suit started walking. We assume to where the field was. He didn't expect to see Grandmaw or she him. Startled, he ran back in the woods. Grandmaw said he was a nice looking man with brown hair average height and weight. A week later My Mom said she remembers talking to a pretty lady at the edge of woods. She was asking things like what she eats, what she celebrates, what do girls like to do, things of that nature. Then one day all the clanging and banging stopped. After about a month, Toni and Terry snuck over to the field and a big circle was burned there. Mind you this was 50's and you minded your own business and kept things only in the family.
