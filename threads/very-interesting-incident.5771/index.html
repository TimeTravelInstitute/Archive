Hello all. First off, I'd like to introduce myself, for I am new to this place.

My name is Johnny and I'm from Pennsylvania and I'm 18 years old. The following story I do not expect any of you to believe. I searched the internet for a place like this to explain what happened and maybe get any help.

Just this past weekend some horrifying happened to me. Something strange and I cannot explain.

The Story:
My friends and I were all at the Mall Friday night. Of course we had a fun night. When we sat down at the food court, a few tables across from us I saw this guy looking at me. At first I thought nothing of it. When we were done, we started walking and all of the sudden I turned around and I noticed the man following me. Again, I thought to myself, "He's probably going to one of these stores in this direction." Here is a brief description of the man: He was about 6'0, he was wearing this black leather coat with the collar popped and his hair was a little lighter than mine. I have dark brown hair by the way. He was older than me too. Like, in his early 30's.

Anyways, I looked back again and he was still following me. I then realized that there was something very familiar about him. My friend finally turned and said, "Hey, that guy looks almost exactly like you. Kinda creepy, huh?" Then, these two guys who looked like some kind of police were following him and they asked him to stop. This guy started running full speed towards us. Naturally, I was freaking out because I thought he was crazy. He stopped only briefly and looked me right in the face as he handed me a piece of paper. As I looked into his eyes I realized...it was me! It seemed like it. It was like looking at my older self. The only thing he said before he ran was, "Go there!" Then he ran right out of the mall doors and the two police people went after him.

I opened the paper and it was a picture of some kind of bridge in these woods. I don't know where it is though and what to do with it. Yesterday, I went to a doctor and talked to my priest about it. What had happened?

Hello, and welcome aboard !


Very interesting account of a strange experience. Funny how some people seem to attract attention in a subtle sort of way.

Different in your case, since it was far from subtle. Would be interesting to review the surveillance tapes if at all possible. Assuming the episode is still on tape.

If I did "go there ", would be very much in stealth mode, and I certainly would consider having a few friends ready to spring into action if necessary.

The aspect I find striking, is how some people seem to be "different". I use to go to a 4 x 4 park, where people would proceed to damage their trucks as they made a game out of trying to cross a deep stream. Crowds would gather to watch these guys wreck their trucks. One day, I felt a "strange" energy and looked to my right, and there was a man, almost just as you described. His whole demeanor was odd. I said hello, and we engaged in a conversation. When he got up and left, it was as though he simply vanished. I never heard him get up and leave. Being near a gravel shelf, I would have thought he would have made "some" noise as he walked away. I was baffled by the man, and I turned to my friend who was on my left and commented about the guy I had been talking too.

My friend told me that it was extremely strange that as hard as he tried to eavesdrop on our conversation, he couldn't hear a word spoken by the man dressed in a black leather biker style jacket. This surprised me, since I could hear him just fine, and he never seemed to be speaking in a subdued voice...my friend was close enough to have heard him, and should have heard every word.

I visited the spot several more times, and the man was there every time. I spoke with him on those occasions, and we even ran little experiments to try and determine IF anyone whom he was NOT talking with, could hear him, and never, did anyone he was not speaking to directly, ever hear a word he said.

The "aura" he gave off, was odd. Something that really can't be explained, except for the feeling he didn't really belong there. Like he was there, but not there, at the same time. I don't know if this makes sense or if anyone else has ever run across someone like this before.

The description of the guy you describe caught my attention, since it is almost a dead-on match.

If the same experience that happened to you, happened to me, my curiosity would get the better of me, and I would make a point of going to the location, but there is no way I'd go without a plan of action.
