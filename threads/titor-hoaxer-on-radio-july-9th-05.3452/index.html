ok well I wasn't purposely posting misinfo but I gotta correct myself, I'm just a bit tired, got back from a movie, and didn't know Titor/Oliver would be a subject/guest on the show because he was apparently booked at the last second.

So I got back from the movies and turned on the radio and it was in the middle of a CALLER's comments... and without paying close attention, the voice of Oliver is similar to the voice of the CALLER, plus Oliver agreed with the caller, that it would be a terrifying possibility, so I accidentally thought they were the same person, that's what happens when ya tune into the middle of a show, but now that I've listened to the entire show I can say that the Reptoid and Nwo comment came from the Caller and not Oliver, don't have the mp3 yet but here's a few parahprases of what Oliver said...

Oliver said:

Multiple dimensions are being taken more seriously now, as a result of Titor's posts. (false, mainstream string theory pre 1999 has 11+ dimensions)

There is now a lot of money being spent to prove more dimensions exist, as a result of Titor's posts. (false, a purpose of CERN was to prove the 11+ dimensions string theory ideas existed long before 1999)

We now know about the unpublished IBM secrets because of Titor. (I would bet money it was published on the net pre 1999, I think someone already found it but I can look it up)

GEORGE NOORY asks OLIVER

"Who is the author of the book?"

Oliver replies by saying "I don't know, maybe a combonation of the family and attorney, or someone else who is associated with them".

NOORY asks "Who is the author??? We gotta track this guy down...."

Oliver, "all trails lead to this attorney" (Larry Harber)

Oliver, "Again the Michio Kaku documentary made Larry was interviewed, and he talked all about it, they called and talked to me also, I've seen the book, it looks like a book, I've got email that people are finding it in borders"

NOORY, "Who's the author?"

Oliver, "I don't know, again that's part of the wierdness that's part of the story, call the attorney, he's got no problem talking about his relationship with the family, he replies to emails, I can give you his email".

That's it.... end...

Is this true, does Larry Harber Atty for the JT Foundation freely talk about his relationship with the family in emails? Is there any evidence of this? Or is Oliver just best friends with Larry because Larry is his attorney and he co-wrote the book?
