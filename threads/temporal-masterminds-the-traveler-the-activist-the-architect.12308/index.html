Jave,


One problem that I have with your general theme is that if disallows real time travelers.


No matter now improbable it might be that a real time travel would come here to post it is a possibility. When I read a would-be time traveler's posts I look for signs of reality. I have absolutely no problem with accepting a plausible assertion of "I am a time traveler", at least to the extent that I would entertain the idea that s/he is real if the story has some real world applicability. So far, however, I haven't seen any time travel saga that comes close to being believable.


I don't, however, want to dismiss out-of-hand every future poster before they even come here, which it appears that your challenge assumes. This is, after all, a forum to discuss the possibility of time travel even if the current state of the science indicates that time travel to the past is not probable (though not theoretically impossible).


On the practical side of the issue I don't want to discourage even the hoaxer. Most of us here post because it is both entertaining and educational. If we set the scene such that not even the hoaxer feels the minimum comfort of attempting to convince us then the site dies a rather sudden and ugly death. I don't know about you, but for me I rather enjoy having a site where we can free wheel, have some fun and debate the philosophy and science of the subject at hand.
