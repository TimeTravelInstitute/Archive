Re: Have U seen the \"RED BALL\"?

Ok I've seen one, I admit it even though everyone laughed at me when I first saw it. I became so obsessed with it that I used to lye down in my back yard next to my pool every night for 4 months watching the sky. I know, I guess I lost it for a while. It affects you like that. 'My kids use to come out and ask me are you ok dad'. 'Sure I'm just watching the stars'.

It started while I was visiting a supplier in Edmonton Canada. I was tired after a long drive (12 hours) form Vancouver. I stopped at a motel in Stony Place just outside of Edmonton. After checking in I went for a walk as it was a beautiful night and I marveled at how clear the sky was. I walk for about 20 yards from the motel into a dark spot which I figured would give me a better view of the night sky.

As I looked up I noticed what I thought were clouds forming above my head. Then it looked more like the northern lights. Wow! I thought, what a great thing to see! The lights started to swirl like a hurricane would look like from space. I saw a small light appear in the center which I figured to be a meteorite. It started to get brighter like it was coming towards me like a ball of fire dropping from the sky. It stopped directly above me what seemed to be about the same distance as a 20 story building. It seemed to just hang there for only a few seconds which seemed like hours to me. It was triangular in shape with 5 triangular shaped lights underneath it which glowed slightly. Then it moved slowly away towards an oil processing plant which I could see because of the flame coming from a tall chimney. I hurried back to my motel and called my home. My son answered the phone. 'I just saw a space ship' I explained. I'll put mum on he said. I think he thought I'd lost it. I told my wife who also thought I'd lost it.

When I came off the phone I called the police to see if anyone else had reported seeing anything in the sky. When they ask me were I was I hung up as I had no idea were this might lead. When I came back to my office in Vancouver I told anyone who I though may take me seriously but found that everyone thought I had lost it until my secretary pointed out that the date on my watch was one day ahead. It was the 16th of November and should have been the 15th. I checked my watch date often that month due to my travel schedule. A week later I flew to LA and as I went through the sensor it peeped. They asked me to go through 5 times and couldn't find anything metal on me and finally let me go. Since the experience I have had a fascination with electro magnetic technology and have been able to look at things in a way that surprises me sometimes, time travel being one. It's like I know something I can't explain even to myself. Ok I know you also see me as a crazy fool, but you had to have been there.

The memory is fading now which surprises me. I can still see the images but not as clear. I did even start to try and explain it in another why since, which also surprises me because there really is only one explanation.

Somebody out there knows this is a true story because 'they' were there also.
