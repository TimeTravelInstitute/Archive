The following are true accounts of a figure that has been seen several times by different individuals. I nicknamed him, " Mr. Krinkle ", from a song by Primus.

The first time I heard about this figure was when I was a security guard at a private community. I was talking with another guard and he told me that one night while he was on duty, he glanced out of the window of the guard station and saw at the end of the road, a tall dark figure. It was dressed in a black flowing cloak and strange black hat. The face was hidden under the shadow of the hat. The figure didnt move, it just stood there looking at my friend. In time he forgot about it, until several months later he came out of the market, and in the distance of the parking lot, there it was, same thing, just standing and watching.

Later, perhaps 10 years, I was talking to another friend, neither knew of each other, each originating from completely different locations as well as life styles. The second friend had just been released from prison, so the odds of them knowing each other are very remote.

One night we were sitting outside and just talking about several subjects, when he brought up the story of how when he was a kid, he decided to slip out his window and ride his bike around the block. He remembers zipping around the corner and suddenly there before him was a tall dark figure, with flowing black cloak, and strange black hat. He described the figure exactly as the other guy did ten years in the past. The figure did the same thing, just stood and watched him as he rode past.

This friend saw the figure a few more times. When he was in prison he mentioned the figure to some of the other guys and a few of them claimed they saw the same figure. They decided to seperate and draw the man they all had seen. When they got back together, the drawings all matched.

Even in the telling of this story, it sends chills through-out.

I am curious if anyone here has seen this figure...and who he might be.
