I believe this topic has been touched on in previous postings, but generally we would know what specific times we have visited before and not visit that specific time again in future missions so as not to run into this problem.

Basically the technology does not allow travel in a 'straight line', but instead in a 'triangle-type' method (sometimes more of a 'N-type).
