The year I'm from I can't remember exactly. I didn't start recording experiences until two months following my first travel. In that time my mind was not ready to "take in" everything I witnessed and learned. I was on the brink of suicide, I was an insomniac; I had a hard time distuingishing dreams from reality. Explaining in words is hard, for I am not a journalist. I know I was born in 1997. My name is Ryan Cokely. After my first travel, I didn't know where (or I should say "when") I was. Right now I'm trapped in 2005. I happen to be in "your 2005" at the moment. I can travel, however, to "different 2005s". I will stay in the current 2005 to reclaim my sanity and to have a hold on reality for as long as I feel neccassary. I will explain it in the way my brain interprets it best. It seems I'm caught on a "horizontal" line so to speak. The horizontal line I'm on happens to be 2005. I got here by travelling "down" on the "vertical" line. The vertical line is a shift in actual years. IE. 2113 down to year 2005. The horizontal and vertical lines are endless. The horizontal line hols the infinite 2005s that I can travel too. What do I mean by infinite 2005s? Every decision made subsiquently produces a "new" 2005. If I please, I can be sent to a parallel universe with a trivial difference as small as me posting this on a different forum. I am in sense moving higher on the vertical line still at the same rate as the earth's rate of time second by second, day by day, year by year, but no faster or slower at the moment. So if I choose to switch parallel universes I might come to this forum and see no post by me tommorow. On the other hand, I might come here tommorow in a different universe, see my post, but also realize that I chose a different user name.

2 weeks ago I left a parallel universe in which all of Europe was Germany! During World War II in that universe no one aided Europe from Hitlers reign. There is a universe for all possible outcomes of any situation of any decision. It is funny how only life forms are capable of creating these parallel universes simply because life forms don't have a constant unchanging "inertia" if you will as does most everything in the arbitrary "beginnig" universe.

I will admit I am afraid to move on to the next Universe spectra on my "horizontal line". I have been lucky so far, and I enjoy this parallel more than any other. I'm terrified that I will end up in a world of oblivion!

Ryan
