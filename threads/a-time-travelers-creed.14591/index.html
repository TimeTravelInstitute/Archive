Friends of EarthTR125.0121


In the last....many years I have seen my share of people claiming to be time travelers. Many somewhat credible, others utterly delirious, still others completely delusional and none real. However, I write today because I did found something particularly odd. Regardless of whatever measures are to be taken by would be time-travelers none of them did anything to protect time, the future or anything in between.


Also, most were rebellious, anarchic and had an attitude to boot. On the following thread I urge you to share with me what you think a time traveler should be.


I believe a time traveler should be smart, open minded, culturally, socially and spiritually qualified since no time period is better than any other and societies, religions and cultures raise and fall like the sun.


They should be brave, intelligent and gentle-people who are capable of listening to others and understanding societies and cultures beliefs and traditions with great clarity.


Finally, they should be resourceful and quiet....not letting others know where they came from.
