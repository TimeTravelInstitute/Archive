The Code

The Wheels

This brings us to the last part to consider. The wheels! The spiral attractor information systems can be placed on wheels, such as the map in one of the pictures above. Spin these and we see a tunnel affect which appears to the senses and within the mind. This raises the question of how the information affects an individual as it can then be viewed at many different speed levels, from extremely faster (turbine jet speeds or faster) to very slow (like a clock), and these speed rates can as well be varied into shifting RPM patterns, to create or control the delivery of information further. The first wheel, map, and code began simple, though when considering how much these simple systems can be integrated into advanced technologies, the imagination is the limit.


Picking up from the above quoted, it has been a while since I opened any threads on the net about this complex topic. At the moment there are some 100+ threads out there, simply Google "The Mind Portal" and you will find many of them very quickly. I stopped for a while for reasons I do not care to discuss, though I will say that it has been enduring for my mind to do. After entering a state of passive meditation I have cleared away many negative suggestions and strengthened my mind so my resolve to carry on with this work is far stronger. I will now discuss some of the things I have observed from using this portal...in two parts.


The first part involves recording one's activities into the map using a matrix system of symbols. I engaged in this process for over a year and managed to completed an entire year, thus, an entire map was filled out. From this I found that I was more productive during this year then any other before or after, the work I did during this time satisfying my sense of purpose in this world more so then the time stemming from other years. Not only that, I have it on record...on the map itself...a testament to my focused effort. The code I used mixed physical, mental, and spiritual activities and conveyed my feelings and my dreams.

I was paying close attention to my life at this time, pondering each moment with more care and I found that midway (180+ days) I began to observe differently with my senses, most notably with my eyes. I was also observing a spinning checkered pattern I scribed into a blank map for experimental purposes when this happened. I began to see energy all around me, coming out of people's heads and objects. The world was changing in so many ways, my perception changing, my mind thinking in new ways. This leads me to the conclusion that when one pays very close attention to their time, they navigate through time to get from one evolutionary point to another quicker. If this is true then we can call this a time machine for that purpose...which would be more powerful then a machine that simply moves the body without moving the spirit. You develop along your giving direction in life faster. Anything you are involved in that you keep track of in the map you will develop with greater speed, as paying attention to your actions and efforts pays off, and the act of writing them down causes the mind to automatically reflect upon them, and so does the act of seeing this information as it collects throughout the year, the reflecting process compounded over time each time you see your time tracks.


The second part involves placing this map onto a wheel and spinning it. This is INTENSE! No one has managed here on earth to go into what I call a spin phase (consecutive spinning from day to day) for more then 50 days, I am the current record holder myself. The first few days not much happens, you ask yourself if your stupid, crazy, foolish, wasting your time. The mind confronts itself and its doubts about how it is using its time. It gets boring from time to time. After about 1 to 2 weeks you begin to feel a difference. You notice that your seeing reality around you slightly differently. Sometimes catching glimpses of moving energy tracing about. Your doubts began to vanish. After about 20 days you notice that time is passing by very slowly to your perception. A minute can seem like 10 or 20. A day can seem like a week. You feel like your moving through time with a sensation of it's passing, while others around you are moving very slowly. Your mind is filled with many, many thoughts. Your senses, ALL of them, undergo a change. I noticed my sense of smell was many times stronger, for example. Myself, along with one other who managed to make it this far noticed what was dubbed the fizzle effect, where the surface of objects appears to have trails of energy burning traces across its surface before closing up like a zipper. Underneath there is emptiness. A sense of vast connection to the universe and self accompanies this experience, and knowledge becomes obvious that was impossible to comprehend just prior.

40+ days there are inconsistencies in time from time to time, the clock might jump forward or backward when your not looking. Some moments there is a warping sensation, as if everything you see is curving. Everything comes to life, the sun, the clouds, the moon, the earth. You feel that they are communicating to you, not with words, but much like animals do with queues. At 50+ days I felt that I was one with everyone around me, that they were pure reflections of me, that there was no separation and the illusion of such was gone. They were simply me in another time...right before my eyes! I didn't manage to go further, there where other things happening in my life at the time and I stopped for a long while. I will resume in the next days, this time to go further and this time knowing in advance what to expect...I am prepared!


I am not making any of this up, it is not a joke, it is the truth as I observed. I am giving my observations here, not the definitions, because no one really knows what happened, there is no where to look and find the answer, only guesswork...even the best psychologists can only guess. In order to KNOW I am conducting research using methods to collect objective data, because anyone can say anything, but it is extremely hard to argue with objective data.
