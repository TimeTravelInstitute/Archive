If disputes emerge on the forum, the following is the dispute resolution process which will be policy here for resolving any wars or conflicts. Please post respectfully and judiciously.

Consider the dispute topic carefully by the weight of the evidence.

If one view has significantly more evidence, describe it as such.

If both sides have comparable evidence, split the topic and describe both sides.

If one view has significantly more evidence, but the other view is still of historical or popular importance, then split the topic and describe the less supported view as less supported or unsupported but still of historical or popular importance.
