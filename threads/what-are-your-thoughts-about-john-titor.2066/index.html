Re: WHAT ARE YOUR THOUGHT'S about john titor...?

My thoughts are that it is either an incredibly well researched, and executed hoax, or JT is/was/will be the real thing.

I see absolutely no definitive proof in either direction. There are a lot of people that sayc 'of course its fake, time travel is impossible' and use that as their closing statement. Then there are others with physics way beyond mine, who tell us that the principles are actually sound, and that there is a possibility that he just might be the real deal.

Now if its fake, and a hell of a good story, I am a little surprised that no-one has actually published any definitive proof, expecially after all of this time, I am also a little surprised that there has been no sign of JT posting here, or anywhere else, even under a different name. His style is fairly easy to recognise, and I tend to feel that any hoaxer would eventually blow his/her cover, because of the sheer impact that the story has had.

So that then leaves the 'JT's story is true' idea. This would mean that in the next few years we are going to continue to advance our knowledge to such an extent that we are going to find a number of answers to currently un-answerable questions, and the universe is never going to look the same again.

So, really I sit on the fence, observing, but also looking for arguements, and clues to authenticate, or finally call the story out.
