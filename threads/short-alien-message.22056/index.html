Binary fusion doesn't seem to have a well defined meaning as yet. However I did find a reference to the term in the cartoon DC universe. A Binary Fusion Generator was a massive energy cannon installed on the second Watchtower. So for all intents and purposes, at present, the term is entirely fictional. But if I were to hazard a guess, I would surmise that it would be a means of obtaining energy, because of the energy differential between two different types of fusion reactions. Of course everything is mere speculation at this point in time. Unless of course someone else has access to information that I failed to find. My search was very cursory.

Binary fusion and fission have reproductive denotations.

Binary fusion is a biological term associated with asexual

reproduction, while binary fission takes place in sexually

transmitted human pathogens, e.g. the parasitic bacterium

of the most common STD among young people, Chlamydia

trachomatis.
