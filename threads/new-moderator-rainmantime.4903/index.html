I am announcing today that RainmanTime has been made moderator of the Time Travel Discussion forum. It is clear that these forums - and the Time Travel Discussion forum, in particular - need more attention than I am capable of giving them. Assigning a moderator is an important step to reigning in sometimes chaotic discussions and enforcing the rules of these forums.

I would like to make it clear that RainmanTime is not being appointed moderator as a punishment to anyone else. He is not going to be wielding a vengeful hammer, seeking to smite anyone who disagrees with him. As moderator, his duties will be to keep things on topic and see to it that things don't become too personal. He assures me that he will not use his authority to attack those who disagree with him.

If disagree with this appointment - or feel that you, too, could be a moderator (we could especially use moderators for the other forums) - then you are welcome to contact me either by e-mail or private message.
