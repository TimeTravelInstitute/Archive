So a human being born on the Moon is an ET; on Mars, or any other planet its the same. Also people tend to adapt to their enviroment. So people from a low gravity world, or orbit, would be taller and willowy. People from high gravity worlds will be short and stocky and those from low atmospheric pressure worlds (like Mars) will tend to be barrel chested for lung capacity. So on and so forth.
