Paradoxes such as this complicate matters dealing with time travel infinitely. Even if some code of conduct existed for time travellers not to make any contact with individuals from the past, there will always be someone willing to defy this for some unfortunate desire for personal gain or other nasty motives. Therefore, giving time travel technology to people from the past will alter the present, causing that technolgy never to be passed on at all. This presents us with a clear paradox: the entire event would have never occured in the first place! One can only imagine the much larger implications of commiting even more profound actions in the past. The deletereous consequences which would result seem to force an inavoidable conclusion: there is some ultimate force somewhere in the universe that will dictate that us primitive beings never achieve time travel, lest we all be destroyed.
