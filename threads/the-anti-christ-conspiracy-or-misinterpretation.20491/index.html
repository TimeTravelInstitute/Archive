The \"Anti-Christ\" Conspiracy or Misinterpretation

I just want to take a few moments an explore all this "Anti-Christ" stuff...

So Jesus was the "son" of GOD and sent to send a MESSAGE to the WORLD...

That probably basically happened...

Jesus was a preacher... He preached GODS word and gave examples on how to live...

He was probably given SUPER HUMAN powers or with GODS direct INTERVENTION affected REALITY in MIRACLOUS WAYS.. Hence, Jesus's miracles... Was it him or GOD listening and watching him as he did his work... Had to tell after you think about it...

His apostle and followers asked when he would return... From one passage and interpreatation is derived that is was basically 2000 years...

Has Jesus himself returned? or has GOD given HUMANITY a different plan?...

Obviously, the PREACHING method Jesus used did not work...

He was hunted down... and cruficied...

And people didn't really follow GODS words or messages...

Many different versions of Christianity emerged... Judiasm grow more powerful... Muslim Islamic religion emerged with some violent enforcement for spreading the word of GOD...

For an entity like GOD who most important commandent is "Thou Shall Not Kill"...

I don't think he happy with the way Islam turned out...

So what does an all powerful entity, GOD, do?

He devises a new plan...

One where example, faith and problem solving are the "convincers" or "inspiration" to others...

It may be one human or a thousand or hundreds of thousands?...

Maybe each new convert spans another...

And this "Satan" business everyone is obsessed about...

Is he a real entity? Is he a fallen angel? If GOD has dominion over all?

Why let an entity like "Satan" exist?

Doesn't make too much sense after you think about it...

Maybe the "Satan" is a story... an allegory...

Maybe "Satan" represents temptation in the Eve and Apple story...

If one has free will... The concept of temptation has to be described somehow?...

Free Will and Temptation?... Basically what the real challenge of LIFE is about?..

How could GOD know you were a GOOD PERSON on EARTH if there was no FREE WILL or TEMPTATION?

So let's review...

Anti-Christ might mean someone who doesn't preach and be a show off with miracles....

Satan may be a bogey man story from the Bible?...

Something to think about...
