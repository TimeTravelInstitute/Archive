Foreword

What KerrTexas perceived as a time machine with an “energy
system,” I experienced as a state of being. Where he dreamt a
computer “capable of sending out ‘roots’ to combine with the
roots of the vegetation,” I preliminarily experienced a sphere
where one can tap the energy of exotic plants as a means of
reaching the above state of being. That state of being led me to
a place of unknown interface. All three (the state of being,
sphere and place of unknown interface) I experienced directly
along with, ironically enough, a scout.

A Place of Unknown Interface

It would be a month after I was revived from a coma before I
would remember the day of my weather-related accident, wherein
I alone was injured. It would be years later before I would next
recall a single dream. Yet, within that time I would have the
distinct impression of my having discovered strange isles of
light where one island of light was means to another island
of light ...&c.

What I would view as isles of light were initially but sporadic
spots of light between blackness. The darkness didn’t frighten me.
So welcome was its quiet restfulness that I could have slept forever.

A light would stir me, then I’d drift back into unconsciousness.
Sometimes my drifting felt more like my being transported.
The motion only more lulled me until the first island of light
came into focus. Unbearable pain caused me to black out.

I gradually found myself seemingly spotlighted while studying
foliage. The plant life was out of this world! Therein immersed,
I was suddenly alarmed by another presence. My being startled
by the aforementioned scout jolted me.

Feeling as though I flew away to safety, I again slept a while
undercover of darkness. I next awoke on another island of light
with symbolic offerings spread before me. An audience of a few
individuals indicated that I should choose one. Noticing the scout
amongst them, I chose the symbol of the mythological character
that I continue to find most fascinating.

The spectators glanced at one another. One moment I was
wondering whether they approved of my choice and the next
instant I was flying again and loving it. I lighted on a few more
islands, saw the scout and took off again.

I rested and, once more, awoke in a place with no interface
where I was instructed in a classroom setting. Afterward, the
scout tricked me into coming to this multiverse. I did not want
to come here, but he said,

“It’s another island of light. You can leave whenever you want.”

Edit: Scratch the last sentence?
Maybe it was I who forgot gravity.
