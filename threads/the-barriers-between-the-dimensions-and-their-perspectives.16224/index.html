We can all see that looking into a tank with fish in it, they would appear completely oblivious to our existence. Not having the comprehension that just outside their 8X11 world of water, lies us. Who can see and monitor every move they make. We may not be able to completely control these 2 dimensional minded beings, but can anticipate their next location that they will swim to, and then use that to our advantage.

As with all other animals, we can see and observe their behaviors through their actions, and determine much of their character. Their needs and wants. Even though not of the same species, it is sensed and observed highly by us.

Therefore, I theorize that the barriers are not just what separates us the between dimensions per say, but through our comprehension and ability to observe something that might really just be right under our noses.

What if higher dimensional beings, can watch us from the perspective we would watch fish swim and live? What would they do to our lives that would make them appear omnipotent, as how we might throw fish food into a fish tank, or ants invading your picnic. They would completely be oblivious to any obstruction that comes along their way, beneficial or not, they wouldn't stop to think or wonder where it came from or how it happened, they accept it as if it was always decided and planed this way by chance. However, we know better .

So, I ask the question; that knowing that this is possible, how can we accept then that our own actions are ours and ours alone, if it's highly possible (not to mention evident in the past) that there can be interference? Some other influences at work, Alien, Time Traveler, Higher Dimensional being. They all have the means of orchestrating and manipulating our lives accordingly, to their perception of what works. NOT OURS!

Free will has always been our delusion.

We aren't, all knowing to see these higher dimensional beings manipulating things in the background, our senses to perceive them are simply just to limited.

It's not until we see things close up like under a microscope that we take notice. Just like when our actions come full circle to us, to indicate that it's too much of a coincidence to be our own doing. I.e. The Double-Digits and other psychic phenomena to that end.

One would conclude, that the barriers between the dimensions are not sealed completely. And that they can find a weakness in them once and awhile to use to their advantage, thus making them capable of interfering in our lives?

But on second note; I think it's only nature or some common law that all intelligent life feel compelled to search out other realms, were our thinking evolves to investigate more of what life consists of?

We have breached the laws of nature before, only to discover that it can support it. We have created technology that assists us in our travelers that exploit the laws of physics. The invention of the ship, automobile and the airplane, and some time later in the future the time machine, have all helped and will help us along to reaching these higher dimensions (comprehension) and exploiting them.

However, we will go to far one day, and using Time Travel to do what we swore we would never do, would create a paradox throughout history that must be fixed. It's a delicate balance that we are facing. Are we really willing to play out being pawns for the rest of our lives to repair and unite existence? The answer is in the paradox it self .
