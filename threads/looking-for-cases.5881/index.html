What you're looking for is what might be called a "Temporal Displacement Experience."

Rather than concentrating on some pseudo-physical hypothesis involving "physically" being displaced in time, you theme seems to be to concentrate on the experience itself, which I think is an excellent approach.

Most of the time-travel claims posted here are based on an implicit belief in "the magic of machinery," --some technological gizmo that will transport the individual through time. This is the modern version of the "magic potion", etc.
