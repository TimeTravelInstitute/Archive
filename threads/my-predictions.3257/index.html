Britain

2008-Tony Blair says that he will not step down 4 years after he said he would. A couple of months later he is assassinated. It is widely suspected that Gordon Brown was behind it but it is never proven even though 2 million pounds were missing from his bank account. He explained this by saying he had been gambling a lot. He is cleared and becomes prime minister.

2009-Alqaeda hacks into ministry of defence computers and hack a missile. It is on collision course with the Houses of Parliament but the military get back in control and blow it up which destroys Big Ben and injures 30 people.

2010-The BNP are elected into government and vow to stop immigration and asylum. They also withdraw from the EU.
A couple of months later Gordon Brown is found dead. He killed his wife and 4 year old child then himself.
He says in his suicide letter that he hired people from the IRA to kill Blair.
Later that year the IRA including the political wing are arrested and the Good Friday agreement torn to pieces.
A few IRA personnel escape and during the "Summer of Terror" 3000 innocent people are killed by bombs and gun fights.
There is hardly any more fighting after that. They could be waiting to consolidate their strength before trying again.

2011- Bad relations with Belgium. They are the first to recognise Flanders as a sovereign state. They also offer military assistance.

France

Spain

2008-A bomb planted in parliament kills the entire government and leaves a couple of opposition party people left. It is thought that ETA committed it. General Orogoza takes control of the country until elections are held.

2009-There is an election. General Orogoza wins. It is widely thought that it was a fix. He vows to start colonising again.

In light of recent events Britain signs a NAP with Spain.

2010-Spain invades Morocco

2012-Morocco falls to Spain but there is still freedom fighters fighting guerrilla warfare

2013-Spain announces for every Spanish soldier killed by the guerrillas 10 innocent civilians will be killed

Dec 2014-The civilian death count is now 60,000 from retaliatory attacks. The Spanish abandon this plan because it is just recruiting more people to the insurgents.
The insurgent attacks continue.


Belgium

2010-Flanders breaks away and they try and get it back which fails.

2011-Belgium is outraged when Britain recognises Flanders as a sovereign state and embargoes Britain. They also have to fight against the British now because they are there.

2013-Belgium gives up trying to get it back after suffering 10,000 casualties since they started to get Flanders back. Britain stays to keep the peace and get Flanders back on its feet.

2009-Russia tries to take Chechnya back.

2010-Sick of the Americans they declare war on them.

They also have to destroy all of their tanks bomber planes, jests,stealth planes submarines, aircraft carriers, nuclear bombs, biological and chemical missiles, short and long range missiles and not have any for 5 years.
