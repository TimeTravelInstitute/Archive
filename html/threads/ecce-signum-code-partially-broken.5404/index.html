Unfortunately I could not get the excel sheet to formulate it automatically so I had to do it by hand. After painstakingly going through them highlighting the ones of possible interest, I then decided to mirror all the words to see if I got any more. Structural Adjustment Loans are also known as 'Sal or SALS' in the world of banking, which strangely enough the IMF made to the Bhutto government. It turned out that the IMF were deeply concerned and unhappy with the way she had handled the money when she received it, so much so that it was one of the reasons she was over thrown and one online report I stumbled on even claimed the IMF may have been involved somehow in her assassination.

This seems unbelievable to me if this was indeed a coded prediction? I also looked up the word 'Seriatim' which is Latin for 'in order or sequence', so what I want to do is try to unravel the other two codes to see if this was just a coincidence. The only problem is that I don't want to spend another two weeks doing it the way I did it, does anyone have any ideas?
