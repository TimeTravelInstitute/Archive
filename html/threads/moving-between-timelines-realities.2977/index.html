Here's where my idea diverges drastically from what I've heard a lot of other people say about world-lines and parallel realities: I do not believe that there are "branchings" per se. Rather, all potential occurrences are laid out across a fifth dimension (which I have succinctly named 'potential') that corresponds to the quantum wavefunction of spacetime, a probability wave, so to speak. The so-called branchings are not discreet. Rather, they are gradations on a spectrum of potential occurrences. Parallel universes are not really universes as we would typically use the term. Rather, the greater gestalt of space-time-potential is a single, unified entity representing the entire potential of an infinite spatio-temporal universe.

Good god, I realize I may have gotten a bit verbose there. I ought to keep that in check.
