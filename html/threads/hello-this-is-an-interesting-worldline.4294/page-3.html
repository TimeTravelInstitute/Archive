Pamela,

Excellent questions. Titor may well be known it is probably that he is just one of many time travelers. Sort of like the "Founding Fathers" of the United States where people generally know of them but don't know exactly who they are or all their names without research. We use the term "Early Travelers" to indicate one of the hundreds of people who time traveled in the 30's. John Titor could have easily been Sean Walsh or Jason Bexley (a couple of early travelers that I know about because we live in the same city). The people of 2006 in another worldline could be talking about Sean Walsh and have never heard of John Titor. I could be the one that actually takes back the information about Titor from this worldline which might not have been known.

Yes, trips like vacations or holidays. Sorry I am so used to using the term 'pleasure trip.' Currently my government has set a limit for citizens taking a time holiday. You either take a group trip or a family trip. The worldline visited has to be around 25% divergent which would typically be a worldline with a breatheable atmosphere and non-intelligent life. There is some concern for messing up worldlines and this is to prevent groups of people from inadvertantly harming one. The reason why I said "non-intelligent" life is because in some divergence "zones," namely between 8-15% the intelligent life isn't always human. For instance there have been stories of worldlines where the Neandertals evolved and the Early Homos died out, or where another species became bipedal and intelligent.

What is recorded is the trip into a worldline so that your return trip will almost exactly follow the same path. "0" divergence is still thought to be impossible. My unit should return me with 0.009% divergence or better. Though not exactly "0" it is so small that I wouldn't notice any difference. I like to think of it as a head full of hair and the difference between a divergence so low is one hair. I could reprogram and go to another time and worldline if I wished. What the unit does on my return trip is return to the "point of entry" that I cam here (May 12th, 2006 19:15.10 UTC) to the closest mili-second then instantly move foward following the line back from my trip. This is how to get back to my worldline with the smallest possible diversion. If I made several trips it adds to the length of the trip as you have to move back and forth from point to point until you reach your original "point of entry." There is only so much air and compressed air with you for a trip and you also run the risk of getting "lost" by making multiple trips.

The 1998 thing is really interesting. It could be that the number has some sort of unknown significance for you.

Our worldline teaches that the United States Civil War started in 2004 when protests against the government heightened and massive arrests of those were arrested for their free speech. All wars do not necessarily involve weapons, although many do, and war can be fought with words. It would be nice if all wars could be a war of words.

I don't really like making "predictions" because what has happened in my worldline occured and what happened there may not happen here. I think many people ignore that point and roast the messenger when something that happened in one worldline doesn't happen in the other.

Judging by what I see here on this worldline the civil war could escalate to a weapons war no matter who wins the upcomming election. No matter which group wins the election one group is going to be angry and may be angry enough to use weapons. I haven't decidied if I am going to stay to watch the result, leave before the election, or a few hours after.
