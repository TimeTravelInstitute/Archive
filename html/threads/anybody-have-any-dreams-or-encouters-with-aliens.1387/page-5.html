Not saying this technique is easy to develop, but :

This technique is to awaken your conscious awareness, and assume control of the dream, once you see your own "dream" symbol.

IF you are able, then next time you have one of these continuing dreams, and have developed this technique as workable, you can consciously ask questions, and/or alter the events of the dream.

There are really only a few texts which promote such wars between men and other's in which all are mostly human looking.

Now as to my theory.

In Jeremiah 4:23-28 in the bible it refers to God looking down on the earth and it was without form or void. It also speaks of cities and man being destroyed, and mountains being moved from shaking. Now, some people have argued that it is in reference to what was going on in Jerusalem at the time. However some people believe it could be referring to a race of man before Adam that was mostly destroyed. Giving reference to Genesis when it says the earth was with out form or void. Then comes along Adam after the first fall of man.

So, after thinking about this, and a lot of research, I wondered...if there was a race of man before Adam, what happened, and could there have been any survivors?

So my research took me to Isiah 14:1-18, which talks about a fall from heaven, scepters, mountains shaking and different other things. It also speaks of the fall of Lucifer from heaven (space).

From here I go to Genesis 4:16&17 which speaks about Cain after he killed his brother and where he went and what he did. He went to Nod and found a wife. So now we have the age old question, where did Cain's wife come from?

So to my actual thorey:

I believe my dream may be an account of the war on earth before Adam, and before it was destroyed the first time. I believe there was one supreme ruler of the Universe (God if you will), and somewhere along the way there was a mutiny between for forces of god and some of his commanders. I think the earth was mostly destroyed by an angry god, but some survived in the end.
