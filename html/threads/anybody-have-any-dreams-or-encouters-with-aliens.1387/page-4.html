lol, I'm really trying not to give my whole book away these were my dreams after all...nahh..kidding.

The order of these dreams are as follows:

1. Evil house
2. House ----> invasion plan
3. Earth changing over
4. Full awareness
5. Battle
6. Struggle for survival

So far I have written over 1200 pages of 10pt font on these 6 dreams, so to say it is a short book would not be correct I suppose. That's why I said I thought about putting each of them in their own book because after reading over some things you've ask me, I've noticed there were details I scribbled on the sides of pages to be added in.

But to get to your specific question with regards to angels. I honestly do not know if I would call them angels in the general biblical sense, as in big wings and the like. These beings were very large, very fair complected, they all had long hair, their eyes could burn a hole through you like they could see inside your soul. They were very powerful creatures, and when they spoke it was calm but with authority. Like even though I was fearful for my life, it was ok because they made me feel protected for some reason. As to what they were wearing it was all ancient looking stuff, like in the first dream they wore those white blouse dress things with a leather belt...think ancient farmer pictures, almost like a long fancy t-shirt with a belt around it. In the 5th dream they wore the same things except they also had on hard looking leather vests on.

They were in my 1st and 5th dreams. In my first dream there were 2, and in my fifth dream there were many more, but not nearly as many of them as there were the dark creatures (aliens). I remember they bleed as we do, but they did not fear death, even though they morn death for each other, but not as a normal human. They morn death through a gang mentality, like they are going to have revenge, although they use much restraint in exacting revenge. They don't just run head first into battle, everything is planned. Very smart creatures as I remember. Obviously all this fighting takes place in the fifth dream, and not the first. In the first they were more of an escort for me back to where I needed to go, so no fighting.
