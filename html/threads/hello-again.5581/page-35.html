IF you have the preview box clicked, then a preview will appear first, and you will have to click on the continue button in the "preview" window for the new thread to be published.

The image posting is a bit tougher. Right click on whatever image you want to post here, and select and click on properties from the fly-out that should appear. I usually will click on my posted image here, re-copy the properties again, then edit the post, and re-do the image without having to go anywhere on the internet to retrieve the property info again.
