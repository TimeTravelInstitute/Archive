I had just enjoyed a picturesque Sunday evening sunset in my backyard here in Huntington Beach, CA. The sunset was so colorful that I even brought my digital camera out to snap a few sunset photos. Here is one that I thought was "epic":

http://www.tree-o-life.org/images/HBSunset.JPG

http://www.tree-o-life.org/images/UFO.JPG

I can't really analyze this all right now. I am just posting up these pics tonight right after this happened. But I saw something really freaky tonight, that's for sure!
