I've actually participated in this phenomenon, for an extended period of my life I had been practicing this. What I found really helpful was listening to particular arrangements of tranquil music and focusing my attention on one particular point, this helps to lull all other thoughts and boost meditation ability. I also conducted these test by lying flat on a soft comfortable surface completely still, having one hand raised while entering this sleep like state is handy, it helps by alerting you if you fall asleep.

The furthest I had ever gotten with this was the entering of strange abnormal sights and sounds into my dreary consciousness. I'm not going to state that Astral Projection is possible but it's worth a try.
A helpful tip with this practice is to desensitise yourself to easy scares, this is because when you mind is in a state of parallel consciousness the things you can witness are so frightening that almost all of the time you will subconsciously want to wake up and rid of these scares.
