penlost,

So far there hasn't been any would-be time traveler who has posted a claim that comes close to making logical sense let alone making science sense. And since Titor's story hit the Internet every would-be time traveler hence has either claimed to be John Titor, a member of John Titor's team or just posted the same story but with another name for the lead character. There's been absolutely no sign that the posters have attempted to be original or especially creative with their literary efforts.

The Titor Saga had long legs, even though the "science" that he posted is pretty awful. And, actually, even the Titor Saga was almost DOA by the spring of 2002.

The only reason that the story stayed alive was because Pamela and I were the moderators of the Anomalies TT forum and we purposely continued to bring the subject up again, each of us for our own reasons. I did it to increase site "hits". The site cost the owner money and only hit counts helped pay the bills to keep the site running.

We kept the story alive and in late 2003 Group Titor decided to go commercial by forming a Limited Liability Company in Florida (The John Titor Foundation) and have an entertainment lawyer run the show. That resurrected the story for another 18 months.

That act did have its own by-product here. You might notice that the TT site on Anomalies has gone silent since interest in Titor dropped off the radar by late 2005. In 2003 this forum was a virtual ghost town at times. For a long time the posts were made by five or six individuals who were simply passing personal nonsense messages to each other. Thankfully, once activity picked up here it wasn't solely for the Titor Saga.
