When I heard about this I was stunned at first. But then I thought he lived a good long life. This is a man who had a career doing something that he loved doing, and that is acting as well as directing. I grew up watching the original Star Trek series, and I remember him all too well in the role of Mr. Spock, but he did so many other TV programs and roles as well in his vast career. He also hosted the syndicated series In Search Of for awhile.
