Greetings! My name is Raymond. I am 31 years old and I currently live in Portland, Oregon, United States of America. I am from the year 2093. I was "sent" back in time and believe it or not, I simply do not know how. The only thing I know is I went to bed one night on December 19th, 2093 and next thing I know I woke up in the same room, same house but just different surroundings on November 9, 2009. I went into complete shock. I have scoured my house for clues as to who I am and realized I have the same name, the same face and same body. I just have a different job, friends and family. All of whom I have no idea who they are. I do not have amnesia and I have not told these people I do not recognize them. At this point I do not know what to do. This all has happened over the last 6 days or so.

At this point I do not know what to do expect to ask for help from anyone who might read this that is the same postion I am in. I know there has to be at least ONE of you out there. Even if you read this years and years from now PLEASE respond and know I will always be looking at this. The only way out has to have an answer. There has to be an answer for the way I got here in the first place.

So far I have been here 6 days and I find it utterly miserable. No offense but the technologies here people think are so great are simply hilarious. I know that sounds condescending but it is the truth.

I read some of these so called "time travellers" and I can tell you they are all lying. Unfortunately, there is no grand secret, no pandoras box or grand war, at least until 2093. After that I do not know. There have been no major epidemics or biblical armaggedon events. Human kind is as it has always been.

I know basic things about history but I do not remember exact details about something that happened 84 years ago. Do you remember who won the yankees/redsox game on June 26, 1926? See what I mean? I do remember things from books growing up and of course things on telenet, what you call television. I can tell you one thing that I find amazing since I have been here is that water is so freely available and does not cost money to drink. I know it seems basic to you now but nearly all the minor and major wars in this future are due to water shortages and water theft.

I can answer any questions you might have but I need help in return. I need to find some one or some organization that specializes in quantam accellerators. I do not know anything about time travel and I do not claim to. However, through some basic research I have found this thing that may help my cause.

I'm not here to get anyone to believe me. I simply need help and if answers to your futures is what you want I will gladly trade for some relevant information on: Quantam Accellerators: Civilian Based Project Contact Information. Basically anyones name that has or is working on such project(s).


Thank You <font color="purple"> [/COLOR]
