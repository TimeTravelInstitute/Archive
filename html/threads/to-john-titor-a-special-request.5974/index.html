Dearest John,

I have last spoken to you about ten years ago.
By now everyone (including me sometimes) thinks you are
just a story some guy made up on the internet.
However I have kept in my heart all of the unexplained things
that have happened concerning you and to this day have no
real explanation.

However, in the least bit chance you were real I am making
this special request.
Remember before you left you asked me a question?
That question was "What can I do for you is there anything you would
like me to do for you while I am time traveling?"

I really, with all of my heart, want you to come and visit me.
I want to see you in person atleast just ONE time.

If you must come in the past just make sure you come AFTER my
dream of you visiting me in April of 1998 Otherwise I may not
accept you because I won't know you but atleast I will be curious
because of the dream.

In this timeline I have not seen you physically yet so I hope to see you in
the near future here.

In my dream I did not see that time machine in the vehicle and I am wondering
now if it was just something you drug along with you and said it was your
time machine.

Your family can get my current address and phone number from Larry Haber.
Please grant this request as it is really the only thing I have ever asked of you.
To meet a real time traveler in person just ONE time and to possibly see a
real time machine in my lifetime.

I have to say it also was a BIG disappointment to never get the departure video
I was SO looking forward to that to see if the time traveling matched my dream.

Yours Truly,
Take care,

Pamela
