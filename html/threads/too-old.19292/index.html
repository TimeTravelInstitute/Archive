The thing about recurring dreams is that they may be an attempt to communicate with yourself.

I once had a continuing dream in which I stepped out of the rear door of a movie house directly between two guys having a knife fight. It was just the right position for them to use me as a shield against one another. If I tried move move one way or the other, they would move to keep me between them.

I had this dream every other night or so for the better part of a week, and it would always end with my waking up (the only way to get out of it). But one day I realized that they were more interested in their fight than they were with me and that the solution to the problem was to simply drop to the pavement, leaving them facing one another.

I tried this and it worked. I repeated this solution several times and the dreams ended. The message here, I think, was that I was blundering into a conflict between two other parties (or forces)and there was no problem if I simply dropped out of it (although I never did find out what the problem actually was).
