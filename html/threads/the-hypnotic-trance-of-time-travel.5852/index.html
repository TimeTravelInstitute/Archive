Fellow friends of EarthTR125.0121

The possibility of Time Travel is of course a reality, but nothing any of us will ever get to see as we all imagine it to be. However, it intrigues me how we are all drawn towards the fundamental ideas of time travel and yet never really stop to ask ourselves why do we want to travel in time in the first place.

The other day, while I was having tea with a friend of mine we were talking about the needs of the Human Race. He gently pointed out that mankind needs to grow past the borders of his mind, to achieve inner as well as outer peace in order to stop war, generate enough kindness as to eradicate poverty and hunger, learn to live properly thus putting away illnesses and most importantly learn to respect the views of another.

When I asked him why was this, his answer was simple. A race like that will not cherish power, nor control, nor even riches, on the contrary... a simple landscape would be enough to fill their hearts for all of eternity. Then will we be able to conquer space, time and the dimensions of the soul. When he taught me this I began to ponder, why is it that we want to travel in time, is it curiosity, or the real need for uncontrolled power and supremacy over the other races. Ask yourselves the same question, maybe it is time to change the approach. It is not whether we discover time travel, is that time travel will discover us...when the proper attitudes are achieved.

Until Later Becomes Now
