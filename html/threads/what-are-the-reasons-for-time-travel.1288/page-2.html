I would remove all misery. This is how it's done: I would send a time machine to everybody and advise them to put one grain into it and send it one second back in time. Then they would have two grains. Now they would put them into the machine and have four grains. This they would repeat until they were rich.

What if somebody would still be unhappy? They could begin psychoanalysis and find out why they were so unhappy. Then they could go back to their childhood and make things right. Because man tends to disagree with himself, after a while there would be quite a few (same) people tempering with some poor family (their parents) and their kid (themselves). This starts to sound like a motif for a sci-fi book. A lot of funny or tragic confusion would follow.

Or perhaps it would be better to advise everybody to do what ever they wished. If they did something they regretted, they could go a while back in time and start over. So if somebody for example partied a bit too much and made a fool of himself, then the following morning he could go back, shoot himself, and take it a bit easier.
