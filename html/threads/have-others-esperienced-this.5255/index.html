In 1993, I began to experience unusual events that seemed to resemble "shifts" in time. I had experienced unusual "shifts" or events all of my life, however, these events in 1993 and on until 2005 became seriously disruptive to my personal life.

I will first of all I will tell you a little about my background so that you can get an idea of why these unusual events disturbe me so. I was raised to believe in the Bible, went to church 3 times a week, was home schooled due to the fact that my mother hated the idea of "evolution" and did not want me to be "corrupted" by anything not Biblical. I was raised to believe that God is the ONLY one who knows the future and NO human or otherwise has power over time. We were not allowed to watch TV except for things like "Grizzly Adams", and "Little House on the Prairy", etc. I was never exposed to drugs or alcohol, and had never even heard of the idea of "time travel", and any "alien" occurences that we heard about, my mother said it was "the devil trying to decieve people". And anytime as a child when I "saw" something before it happened (mostly what can be discribed as "de javu"), I was told either that God had "revealed" it to me or that the devil was trying to trick me (which ever was the most convinient at the time). So you can see that I had no ideas that even remotely resembled the possibility of "time travel".

In the summer of 1993 (I had just turned 19) - all day I had felt strange tingling sensations, metalic taste in my mouth, and "out of touch" with things around me. I would reach for something and it would seem like it was "in the water" somehow, like my eyes were looking through water but when I would reach for the item it was not where my eyes told me it was. It was "off" somehow. Then at about 1 AM after not being able to sleep, I was sitting on my couch trying to watch TV, Finally I turned it off and thought to go to bed but then as I was looking at the TV, which was setting kitty corner accross from me, the only way I can explain this is that the walls started "melting", or "warping" sort of bending in. I became very dizzy, disoriented and terrified! I covered my head and closed my eyes but when I opened them everything was still "warping". I became very sick and all I could do was sit down and "wait it out". The next day I went to the doctor and they ran tests. I was in perfect health.

I would not even be speaking of this event had the experience not happened several more times over the course of 12 years. Each time an event like this would happen the next day things were "out of place". People were not the same, sometimes there were people I knew that no one ever heard of who seemed to have "disapeared", no one remembered them except me. Buildings would be different (or not even there), major things moved, and events that seemed to have "never happened" that I seemed to be the only one who remembered them, sometimes the reverse was true, everyone else remembered events and I had no recallection of them. This happened often when I was a kid too however I thought it was because my mom or others were so stressed out that they "just forgot".

This is the first time I have posted this experience on a site like this. Since 1993, many events have happened that have lead me to believe that somehow I am "attached" to some "temperal disturbance". I don't know how this would or could happen or why. I have "memories" of how my children look or act "from the future", I have "seen" or "walked through" houses that I would live in 2 years in advance, and I have "remembered" things that had not happened yet. This is more then a "de javu" thing. Although that does happend often as well. Sometimes I change things, sometimes I let them stay the same as I saw them to be. I do not believe myself to be "psychic" because when an event happens it is more then just "a vision", it includes physical symptoms. As I said before, I have no tumors in my brain, and am in "perfect health" according to doctors.

I fianlly decided to post because I was wondering if there are others who have experienced similar events?
