We buy a house in Szombathely and a trabant. My father does not become an espionage so in 1981 we can go to the states for my grandfather to have the carotid artery surgery. We go to san diego in november of 1981. We stay for xmas in NJ: my great grandparents, my parents and myself. In 1982 i start my hobbies. We buy a vacay house at lake Balaton. My father gets into a car accident with his mother and they die. My Mom gets remarried and has more kids. The summers we spend at the lake windsurfing and sailing. I go to Karolyi Mihaly high school in Budapest so I learn Spanish and German. In 1994 summer i go to Argentina to study spanish, tango and to tank up on B12. We come to the states again in 1995 for me to take toefl and sat tests. In 1996 i come to Rutgers. I take German, Spanish, Russian, French and Italian beside the bio classes. The summers i spend home and in those countries. I get married for the greencard. In 2000 i go to Harvard for 8 years. I meet TK. In 2008 we move to Miami. In 2011 i finish. We travel the world for a year. In 2012 we go to Hungary for the festivals. We have a real wedding. Then i open up a functional medicine clinic in Coral Gables. We start trying for baby #1.
