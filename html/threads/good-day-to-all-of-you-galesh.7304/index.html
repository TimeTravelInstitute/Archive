Good day to all of you, Galesh!

My generalities are unnecessary.

I am a time traveller from الدوحة, also known as Doha, Qatar, but I am not originally from the arabian peninsula. I come from the year 1479, or 2057, if the Gregorian calendar is preferred;

I know many time travellers that were just impostors, have appeared on the web in recent years; from what I have been told, I am the first one allowed to talk on the web.

I am here because I think that this web forum is the best way, to describe how will be the life in future; offering this kind of informations to governments or newspapers would be totally useless.

The first economic power will be the Arctic, administered in fair portions of land from 5 countries, that are the permanent members of the United Nations Security Council.
From the territorial point of view, there will be many adjustments.
For example, the People's Republic of China will have acquired the territories corresponding to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Lao People's Democratic Republic. The Republic of India will have acquired the territories corresponding to the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, the Kingdom of Bhutan, the People's Republic of Bangladesh and the Republic of the Union of Myanmar. These 2 are just examples of the many adjustments that took place around the world.

In early 20s, U.S.A. will have declared default, changing currency and economic system, seizing gold reserves held in the name of other countries, coming out from these turbulent years politically and militarily resized.
In late 20s, the European countries will have completed the integration started many years before, with one currency, one government and one army. All the countries of the ex URSS (excluding the Russian Federation, of course) will enter before or after, unlike the Republic of Turkey, whose candidacy will never be officially endorsed.

In 2027/2028 will be developed a new form of energy, completely renewable and environmentally friendly. I can not say more about this, apart the fact that the discoverers of this form of energy, officially scientists from the CERN, will actually be engineers from the U.S.A. Through reverse engineering applied to unknown flying objects , they have discovered their functioning, although not totally.

During these years, there will be many unions between different governments, forming states that could be defined as federal; this will happen in South America, between the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela and other neighbors, or between countries of central Africa.

This is a list of information that I can talk to now.
1. The first time travel have been made in order to kill Adolf Hitler's mom in 1888; murder that led to devastating results in the 70s, forcing a second time travel in order to stop that murder. This first time travel officially took place in 2035.
2. The third time travel have been made in order to kill محمد البوعزيزي , known in western countries as Mohamed Bouazizi. Again, this choice led to devastating results in the 20s of the new century, forcing again a new time travel in order to stop that murder.
3. Time travelers are mostly government agents from offices that do not officially exist. Currently, the technology for time travels are fully known only from 6 countries. The first country to discover this technology were the U.S.A.
4. Apart from these governments' offices, there are three illegal groups that own the technology for time travel; I am a member of one of these.
5. The world population will reach 9.8 billion in 2057.
6. The Republican Party will win the presidency in 2016 and 2020. This will lead to a rather problematic relationship between the U.S. and the Russian Federation. On the contrary, the relations between the U.S. and the Republic of Cuba will become more and more friendly, mainly for economic reasons.
7. In 2048, will be launched the first mission to Mars with astronauts.
8. By the year 2057 will be discovered five planets, whose distance from the earth will be less than the thousand light years, suitable to host life as we understand it.
9. In 2057, islamic terrorism still exists.
10. By the year 2057, life expectancy in the most advanced countries will be of about 127 years.
11. The way to greatly reduce the risk of cancer and how to stop the worsening of the medical situation in case of cancer have been discovered before the advent of the new century, but will be widely publicized only between 30s and 40s.
12. The fight against drugs, unnecessary and wasteful, have been completely useless until the 40s. Since the early 50s, many governments, in particular in Europe and Asia, have realized that the only way to fight it is to establish legal monopolies, where the sole vendor is the state.

Thanks, Galesh!
