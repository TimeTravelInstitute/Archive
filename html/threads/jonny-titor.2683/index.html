OverLrd,

You're correct about the pronunciation. It is T-eye-Tor.

His original name was Time Travel_O here on TTI. TTI allowed anonymous login until sometime just before he stopped posting. "Time Travel_O" was a play on words meaning "Time Traveler Or not a Time Traveler" which went along with his statement that he preferred that we not believe his story.

But Art Bell's BBS, Post-2-Post, didn't allow anonymous login. To register you had to provide a first and last name and a valid email account. To register on Bell's BBS he used "John Titor" (the last name being the contraction of Time Travel_O). He used his AOL email account, Sanguina@AOL.com.

BTW: Though he said that he was leaving in March of 2001 the AOL account remained open until the summer of 2001. It was closed in August of 2001. I used AOL at the time and sent email to the account. It was read but unanswered. Someone was using the account for several months after March 2001.
