Genko,

I don't know how Voyager described Larry Haber so I'll give you some background:

Larry is an entertainment industry lawyer. He helps filmmakers put together corporations and limited iability companies so that they can make movies. He's been a top corporate lawyer for Disney Corporation and Universal Studios and he's been the executive producer for one Hollywood film.

When the John Titor Foundation was first formed it's corporate address was the same as Larry Haber's law office address. The law office was located in a building in Celebration, Florida that housed a film studio, sound recording studio and graphical arts studio. Larry was also the attorney for the business, VISI Corp, that owned the three studios.

We know that the John Titor book and the John Titor radio program promotional CD were produced in those studios.

But the interesting connection is Celebration, Florida itself. Larry Haber was the first person to move into Celebration after Disney Corporation built the town next to Disney World.

Celebration was built by Disney Corporation be their vision of "tomorrow's" perfect little techno-village - an extension of Tomorrow Land in both Disney World and Disneyland. When they built the village they wired every building, house and apartment for the Internet.

John Titor, as "TimeTravel_0" made his first appearance on an Internet forum here at Time Travel Institute. We didn't know why he chose this site - and probably never would have figured it out.

In 2003 we learned about the John Titor Foundation. It's address in Celebration, Florida appeared on the Amazon ad for the book. When I saw the name of the town it sparked a vague memory because I had a feeling that I'd seen the name of the town somewhere before. After a little research I discovered that Celebration is located next to Disney World. It took a few weeks and then it hit me...I'd seen the name Celebration here.

At the bottom of each page on the forum there's a "Contact Us" hot link. It's no longer there, but back in 2003 there was an ad for "Magical Mouse" on that page. That linked to the site owner's Internet advertising agency page. The site owner here had several Disney webpage advertising accounts. There was also a hotlink to one of the "minor" Disney related webpages. The site was www.34747.net . "34747" is the postal ZIP code for Celebration, Florida. The owner of this site was the original web designer and System Administrator for Celeration's community website. He was also the Moderator for the community BBS forum. When I went to the "34747" website there was a hotlink to this site on Celebration's webpage.

So, why did John Titor choose this site for his first appearance? The answer is because he was a resident of Celebration and found the link to this site on their community website.

BTW: The owner of this site, Raul (MOP), isn't John Titor and he didn't play a role in the hoax. Also, the hotlink for "34747" is dead. The site was taken down a few years ago.
