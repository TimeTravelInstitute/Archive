For anyone attempting to build a time machine, I say, Why bother building and testing and experimenting. You can prove almost instantly if you will ever create or have access to a time machine.

Step 1 : Get a Safe, with a combination memorized and known only to yourself.

Step 2 : Set a specific date/time when you will not be around the safe for a day or so.

Step 3 : Make up your mind that you will create/invent/steal/borrow a time machine at some point in the future.

Step 4 : Get the blueprints/diagrams/instructions for building the machine.

Step 5 : Travel back to the point in Step 2, put the instructions in the safe.

Step 6 : You have just set the specific time and wandered around for a day or so, go back to the safe, open it up.

Conclusion : If you find the instructions for the time machine then you have succeded. If the safe is still empty then you have failed and you will not build/create/steal/borrow a time machine in your lifetime.
