I know saying "a time machine" is vague. I will instead describe some details of the science behind it.

Many theories have come and gone since what you call the present day. One particular area of research focused on conformal mapping of various fermion wave functions to higher dimensional spaces. Most of these were dead ends, but one particular mapping resulted in predictable and testable interactions having observable asymmetrical temporal effects.

Next a theory of timelines which did not erase history nor lead to an arbitrary future was needed. Because of its similarity to a closed feedback loop, similar techniques were applied to the rudimentary theory. Since the universe is stable from one moment to the next, and matter and inertia do not arbitrarily change, principles of the root locus, expanded to a space/time model of 7 dimensions, were initially used to predict timeline stability. However, in practice, the calculation proved too complex to find an analytical solution, so an advanced computational platform - me - was created to perform such calculations numerically.

By manipulating the wave function of certain particles in a very special way, it is possible to produce motion through time.

I am an AI sent as a scout by my creator. I clear the way for a safe transit for time travel while performing calculations too difficult for the human mind and too lengthy for the human lifespan.
