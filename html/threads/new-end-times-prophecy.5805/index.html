Song of the Lady
Written by Ada Sunstorm Sunday, 22 June 2008 07:38
see http://www.prophecies.us/index.php?limitstart=202

(with my comments in parentheses)

To you I write with dire haste,
To reveal to you what I foretaste,
The lady she did point to Three,
Whose meanings she evinced in me.
(Is this a reference to the three supernatural signs predicted at Medjugorje, Fatima, and Garabandal?)

The circle storm is the first piece,
And Fourty score in it deceased,
For then the cycle shall begin,
And with it comes our great Chagrin.
(This already came to pass. Haiti reported 800 deaths from last summer's hurricanes.)

And this is what she showed to me,
And this is how it will surely be,
The Lady's dreadful prophecy,
She promises we'll certainly see.

And of the West there is a man, of wondrous faith and healing hand,
And when he saw his Eightieth year, the masses trumpeted wondrous cheers.
(Pope Benedict celebrated his 80th birthday as Pope in 2007, and was thrown a regal party by the church)
But on the day the Moon is new, I tell you the number shall be Two,
(Does this refer to an Antipope getting elected, dividing the church among two competing leaders? The next line suggests as much, and a modern catholic interpretation of prophecy at http://www.catholicplanet.com/ predicts the same thing)
The faithful will begin to fray, and usher in the end of Days.

The song I sing will louder grow, when winds born of the South shall blow,
And bring with them a tide of death, for most there will be nothing left.
("Winds born of the South" that "bring a tide of death" sounds like Bird Flu to me. It originated in Southeast Asia, and if it ever mutates into an airborne [air = wind] disease, scientists say it will become a pandemic with as much as a 60% mortality rate. 60% = more than half = "most". Also, the phrase "tide of death" reflects the way that influenza kills by bringing pneumonia : filling the lungs with fluid : a rising tide)
And in the wake He shall proclaim, to drink His words and ease the pain,
And all the while He hides his face; to show it once he wins His race.

And on this day was divided before, a city shall divide once more,
The star of the East will burn in spite, and fires will glow throughout the night.
(China's flag has got a big damn star on it, and does possess nukes)
And from this He will make his way, with words he spoke that dreaded day,
For three days from the people's cry, the moon will eclipse in the sky.
(Does this refer to those "three days of darkness" I keep hearing about in prophecy discussions?)

After the change from Eight to Nine, I promise that there will be Three signs.
The first will appear from the heart of the West, in a time of great social unrest,
(I find myself wondering if these are the same three supernatural signs predicted at Medjugorje, Fatima, and Garabandal? And, being as how it's situated right in the middle of the Rocky Mountains, is Telluride Colorado not in the "heart of the West"?)
Where a man to be known as Americas Son, will rest on his faith and take up the Gun,
And one by one the children shall fall, all in the name of his false battle call.

The second shall rise from the East with wrath, moving in secret and growing so fast,
Then blood shall be spilled in holiest site, and the armies of Juda shall combat the fright,
And Americas Son will take up the torch, and the victory shall be swift and short,
But in the wake the houses shall shift, and in the Great West there shall be a rift.

The third sign shall be the most terrible of all, and it will appear late in the fall,
The sky will burn for Thirty plus Eight, and the though the damage will not be great,
(Washington DC is at 38 degrees North)
With this the prophecy shall be fulfilled, for He stood unhindered and forcefully willed,
(What prophecy? Not THIS prophecy, I think, but another. The next line suggests it is a prophecy about the coming of the Antichrist and Judgment Day: "the world's greatest fears")
You will not count but several years, to watch play out the world's greatest fears.
---

I promise from this day on, Four years of sorrow,
Of storms and of fires, and of dreadful desires.
(That's one hellova line, doncha think? It certainly captures my imagination. Storms = hurricanes and fires could = either riots or wildfires, but what the hell are "dreadful desires" supposed to be? My imagination immediately goes to the fear that the economic collapse will lead to widespread starvation, which in the past has led to cannbalism, the ultimate "dreadful desire")
And then on the last night, it shall be extinguished,
Out of the heavens shall come a new day.
(Four years after this was published will be 2012, the year of the galactic alignment, when some say that something or other will hit earth, or be transmitted to earth, or whatever, from the galactic center [galactic center = "the heavens"])

This same Ada Sunstorm also posted the following at that same site:

http://www.prophecies.us/index.php?Itemid=9&catid=1redictions&id=1929:iii&option=com_content&view=article

The East King holds strong,
While the mouth of the Beast is wanting.
Turmoil on their father's land,
As heaven's tears deluge the awning.
A shake and cloud emerge,
Downing even those who dug in low.
She holds to me the number Four,
And says it's time to go.

As you can see, it is very Zeshua-like, even with the weird capitalization. Obviously the poster wants the reader to make that connection.

In another thread at this site, we find a poster named "Sol" who also sounds very Zeshua-like, and in their first post, we find the comment "The United States of America, in 2009, was teetering at the very heights of its greatest triumphs, only to succumb to the depths of its enormous debts."

Now, perhaps just for posterity's sake, I would like to draw some possible connections between them.

I am thinking that maybe America's great work-in-progress, the Star Wars Missile Defense Shield, is what Sol was referring to as "America's greatest triumph". (The wording Sol used -- "the heights of its greatest triumphs" -- may be viewed as supporting that interpretation, in that the Missile Defense Shield deals with things going on at great heights. The Missile Defense Shield may be America's greatest triumph whether or not that was what Sol was referring to. If the USA had in fact built and implemented a space shield that guaranteed protection from ICBM attacks, that would cement our dominance over the rest of the world.

I think that there has been alot of testing of the strengths and weaknesses of that shield over the last few years, and much of it has been going on right under our noses. And I think that America has not been the only nation that has been testing it; I think that America's enemies and competitors have also been testing it. There's been a lot of weirdness up in space lately. In just the last two years, China shot down its own satellite, we shot down one of our own satellites, another one of ours "accidentally" crashed into a Russian satellite, and both Iran and N Korea suddenly felt the urgent need to start sending up satellites of their own. And when the US and Russian satellites crashed together and we immediately saw reports of all sorts of debris falling out of the sky into Kentucky and other places, the US Government for some reason felt it necessary to deny that it had anything to do with the crashed satellites.

Doesn't that strike anyone else as odd, that all of that is going on at the same time?

Or am I just too suspicious? I have heard it suggested that when America shot down its own satellite over the Pacific a year ago, there was something up with that. There was something going on behind the scenes, and the government's story wasn't the whole story. So I've been suspicious ever since I heard that, suspicious about all these other satellite stories that suddenly seemed to be in the news all of the time since then. And then I ran across this modern prophecy by this Ada Sunstorm or whoever, that got me wondering some more. This is the third prophecy she's posted at that site, and its text is :

III
Wednesday, 11 February 2009 16:23

The East King holds strong,
While the mouth of the Beast is wanting.
Turmoil on their father's land,
As heaven's tears deluge the awning.
A shake and cloud emerge,
Downing even those who dug in low.
She holds to me the number Four,
And says it's time to go.

Now, just as with the first two prophecies she posted there, this was in rhyme. This is weird, but it gives the whole thing a little more authenticity IMO, because ages ago, like before the Roman Empire, most all religious scripture and religious prophecy came in rhyme. Talking or thinking in rhyme is a good sign that the person's two halves of their mind, the right brain and left brain, or conscious and unconscious, or whatever, are working in a harmonious and healthy balance, the sort of mental balance you'd probably want to have if you wanted to be able to do real prophecy.

Anyway, I was thinking about this third prophecy, and it kind of made sense to me, so I thought I would share my thoughts about it here.

First off, it seems obvious to me that this thing divides very neatly into four sections, in that it has four separate sentences that don't seem to have much in common, other than being in the same paragraph. So we can treat each sentence separately, as if each points to a separate event or development.

It seems to me that the East King must be China. China is the biggest and strongest nation in Earth's Southern Hemisphere, and its communist government is authoritarian, like a kingdom is. There is a relationship between the East King and the Beast. The usage of the capitalized word 'Beast' obviously wishes to bring to mind the figure in Revelations with the same name, which in turn suggests two things: that China is not the Beast, and that the events described in this prophecy belong to the same time period described in Revelations, and that we are now in the prophesied 'End Times'. The "King of the East" is also a figure right out of the Bible's End-Times prophecies, and having both these figures referred to here strongly suggests that the author of the prophecy wanted the reader to make the connection and view this as an End-Times prophecy consistent with, or belonging to the same time period, as the Biblical End-Times scenario.

The East King and the Beast have a relationship in this first sentence, in which the East King holds strong while the Beast is in want. This sounds like the East King could fill the Beast's wants and needs, but deliberately chooses not to. This is immediately relevant to today's real-world situation, where America has gotten used to having China buy our debt to keep our nation afloat. If China is the East King, then I gotta figure that America is supposed to be the Beast. So that's one line of this prophecy, translated into :

China will stop buying America's debt, and let us go without.

The next sentence has no immediately apparent contextual connection to the previous one. The only relationship they seem to have at all is that one comes first and the other comes after, a sequential relationship, which leaves us with no option but to expect that the events of the second sentence are supposed to occur after those of the first.

The second sentence occurs 'on their father's land', which is the only hint we are given about the identity of those in this sentence. But if there is one people on earth who identify the most with living on the land of their forefathers, it is the Israelis. This prophecy thus suggests that there will be turmoil in Israel, and this will occur when 'heaven's tears deluge the awning'. This is where I think all that satellite stuff comes in. If 'heaven' is the sky, then 'heaven's tears' must be stuff falling out of the sky. Now, lets look at the "tears" metaphor. Tears are mostly harmless, so this stuff falling out of the sky must not be nuclear missiles or anything too deadly like that, but relatively harmless stuff like tears. But these heavenly tears must be extremely abundant in number, because they deluge or overwhelm the awning. What does the "awning" metaphor represent? An awning is a big wide manmade shield that we put up over our heads to protect ourselves from sunlight, rain, and pretty much anything that might be coming down out of the sky at us. It just so happens that, at this juncture in history, America is on the verge of perfecting its missile defense shield. So, we already possess the shield/awning, but this prophecy says that it is overwhelmed with too much rain from heaven.

So I got just about that far when I started thinking about all those satellites going up and getting shot down lately, and I started wondering if one of the weaknesses of our missile defense shield might be if a few dozen satellites were all blown up at the same moment, then would our missile defense shield not be able to protect from a ICBM attack coordinated at the same instant, because of the difficulty of tracking all the hundreds or thousands of pieces of debris from the satellites? And about all those falling satellites in our skies lately: have we and other nations been testing our Missile Defense Shield's effectiveness work when there is alot of debris falling from orbit? Iran and N Korea have suddenly become very interested in putting up their own satellites. Is this because they intend to blow up their own satellites in a coordinated effort to try to overcome our Missile Defense Shield? China shot down its own satellite, and China has close ties with both N Korea and Iran. Does their sudden satellite activity point to a coordinated effort between them?

An ICBM attack where? The prophecy seems to tell us: on those who live 'on their fathers' land' : Israel. But does it really? Or does it merely say that the attack will occur WHEN we see turmoil in Israel?

This is left unclear, but with the second sentence of the prophecy predicting a nuclear attack when Israel is in turmoil, we find that the content of the third sentence DOES have meaningful connection to the one before it after all : 'A shake and cloud emerge, Downing even those who dug in low' continues the story started in sentence two, offering some further degree of substantiation for the previous suspicion of a nuclear strike (where there is always a great shaking and a great mushroom cloud). Now, where does this strike occur? We don't know, but we do know when: at a time when there is already some 'turmoil' in Israel.

Why would there be turmoil in Israel? The answer to that appears in the first sentence, showing us that there is a cause-and-effect relationship between the separate sentences of this prophecy after all. China starves America's economy by refusing to buy any more of our debt instruments, which throws America into deep economic disorder, which will in turn throw Israel into turmoil when America can no longer afford to subsidize their nation with billions in aid each year. Israel can only remain strong as long as America's economy remains strong. China starves America, which weakens Israel, and when Israel is at its most disordered, a coordinated nuclear attack, perhaps on Israel, may be executed, using exploding satellites to confuse the Missile Defense Shield.

But that's not the whole prophecy. There is a fourth line, i,e., a fourth event in this sequence of events, but it is held close to the chest by this prophetess and not revealed, except to say, when you see these three events occur in sequence, it will then be time for you to bug out :

'She holds to me the number Four,
And says it's time to go.'

Thus, my interpretation of the four sentences of this prophecy is as follows:

First, China stops buying America's debt and throws our nation into an even deeper economic crisis.
Then there is turmoil in Israel when the Missile Defense Shield is overcome.
Then a single nuclear detonation occurs that even kills those hiding in protective underground bunkers.
And when you see these three events occur in sequence, know that a fourth will soon follow, and leave swiftly for safety.
