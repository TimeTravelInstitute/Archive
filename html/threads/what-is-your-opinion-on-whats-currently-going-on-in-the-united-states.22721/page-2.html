I believe that there are agents assigned to an investigation who are doing the work. Therfore, just because Comey was fired, does not mean those agents packed up all their stuff, put whatever they collected into storage and are like, "woo hoo, we're out'a here" and went on an extended vacation. Now, if it comes to light that the new director issues an order that any and all investigations were to be halted --- then maybe I would start squawking too. My opinion only - but - the people screaming about it, are being illogical and unreasonable.

On a more humorous side, I have a friend that is an experienced and skilled hunter. Sitting around one night, drinking some beer with a group of folks, he says as a matter of factly, that if and when you hunt for wild Turkeys, always aim behind em. Because the way wild Turkeys are physically structured, they can only fly backwards.

I've never really seen a wild Turkey fly, have never been told how a wild Turkey can fly, and know that they are large and cumbersome creatures.

That they can only fly backwards, from the inflection and expression of my friend, you'd believe he was serious and telling the truth. What I find interesting about this event, is that I would bet money that some of the people that heard him say that, never did any research themselves and on "good authority" are passing it around if and when hunting wild Turkeys, always aim behind em, because wild Turkeys can only fly backwards.
