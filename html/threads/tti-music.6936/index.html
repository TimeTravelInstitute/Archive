Hi, Gpa.

It was a rhetorical question, which was to suggest how fortunate are we
to so conveniently be able to enjoy eclectic music from around the globe.

The guidelines for posting music herein are both broad and simple.
Anything that remotely pertains to TTI (a section, discussion, reference,
comment, something of interest to a TTI member...&c.) will make for
great ambience. Members, by submitting instrumentals/songs/playlists,
can set a mood for their reading pleasure as well as threads and comments.

Post your favorite Steppenwolf songs. John Kay's lyrics reflect the fact that
he doesn't take freedom for granted. From his biography, "[...He] grew up
trapped behind the iron curtain. His first distinct memory is a daring night-
time escape with his mother to West Germany[...]"

All,

All genres of music appreciated.
