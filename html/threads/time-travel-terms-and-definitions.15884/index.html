Time Travel: Terms and Definitions




absolute space:


The First Dimension. Height,


The Second Dimension. Width,


The Third Dimension. Space Depth(See Stereoscopic Cameras and Infra-Red Vision),


The Fourth Dimension. Time(imagine flipping height-width-depth pictures to make a movie)


The Fifth Dimension. Memory(magnetic light waves that rebound from material to endlessly travel into space)(The Ghost Dimension)


absolute time:


See collective consciousness and subconsciousness. A mutually agreed upon reality that everyone within a social group shares.


A Nixi Clock data coupled with an Atomic Clock. Atomic decay as a reliable measurement of the passage of time.



curvature of space or spacetime:


The fact that time is linear but also curves as it approaches various densities of gravity wells. A drop in water creates circular ripples. This happens on a macro-scale. Time-lines and World-lines converge in nature and can be converged artificially.



exotic material:


A rare natural or artificial element with a heavy density. As fuel injected with electrons, this element's density can be controlled. Thus the gravity well it generates can also be controlled. Time-lines and World-lines can then be navigated. Element 113.



field: The sea upon which converging surf waves of time-lines and world-lines interact.

geodesic: These are linear and curved vectors of pilot waves upon which time travellers navigate the ergospherical gravity-space-time field.

gravitational time dilation: Converting Element 113 to Element 115 and back again through the application and subtraction of electrons, thus increasing or decreasing the surf wave gravity wells. This brings Time Line 2036 of World Line A to Time Line B 1999 of World Line B. It is impossible to navigate to Time Line A on World Line A.

gravitational wave: See above.

mouth: Less like a mouth or a hole and more like a hill or wave. A singularity or black "hole" is not a hole. It is a super dense marble creating a ripple in the pond which is this sea of gravity, time and space.



perturbation: the distortion of matter, a time slip, or a gravitational anomaly as a result of time travel. Lost time. Changed memories. Divergences betwixt 2 World-Lines with 2 Time-Lines measured in DELTA.

singularity: See gravitational waves, gravity waves, pilot waves. On a macro-scale and micro-scale. A super cooled element is super heated. This creates a miniature condition similar to a macro black hole.

spacetime diagram: A simple graph of Vertical World-Lines and Horizontal Time-Lines. Contains virtual and augmented holographic controls and emergency analogue stick and button controls. Controls the electron bombardment of Element 113 increasing and decreasing the gravity well allowing specific time-lines upon an edited World-Line to be selected, viewed, and travelled to.

time reactor: A radioactive element contained and suspended within a magnetic cylinder that materializes time bubbles and defensive shields. As opposed to a fusion reactor that powers the anti-gravity propulsion and suspension. 


world line: an alternate universe other than your own. A universe which may be a clone of your own. The world in which Time Travellers settle to enjoy the editing that they have done.
