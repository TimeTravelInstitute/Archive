Terms of Service

Acceptance of Terms

The Time Travel Institute Forum is provided to you free of charge under these Terms of Service. By visiting any portion of the Time Travel Institute's website's and/or participating in services you are indicating your agreement to be bound by these Terms of Service.

This Discussion Forum shall not be liable to you for any breach of these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these Terms of Service please do not view the site or participate in the services.

This forum is moderated according to these Terms of Service. When you created your account, you agreed to these terms so it is important that you understand what is expected and what is not allowed. Although we have included specific Rules of Conduct below to help you stick to the Terms of Service, it is important to review the terms and ask questions if you don't know what something means.

Posting in Time Travel Institute forum is a privilege, not a right. Posting privileges can be suspended or revoked at any time by the Administration with or without notice.

By using the Time Travel Institute Forum, you are agreeing to abide by the Terms of Service. The purpose of these Terms of Service are to make the Discussion Forum a safe and friendly environment for everyone.

These Terms of Service may be updated at any time with or without notice. The Administration of Time Travel Institute may supplement these Terms of Service with additional terms pertaining to specific content, services or events

Any information posted within this site does not necessarily represent the policies or opinions of the administration.

Any material or information posted on this site should be read with diligence and care.

Disclaimer of Warranties

You expressly agree that use of Time Travel Institute Forum and all services and events is at your sole risk. The Administration of Time Travel Institute makes no warranties to the content, service, events or availability of the Discussion Forum.

Termination

The Administration of Time Travel Institute reserves the right, at its sole discretion , to immediately, without notice suspend or terminate your registration/participation with or ability to access the Discussion Forum and/or other services or events provided to you.

The Administration of Time Travel Institute may modify or discontinue the web site, services, or events provided to you without notice and without liability to you.

Inappropriate Content

Profanity, obscenity, insults, personal attacks, accusations, derogatory or defamatory comments, or other abusive or inappropriate content is not acceptable.

Using asterisks, characters, or alternative spelling to bypass this Rule is considered to be the same as posting the actual inappropriate word or content..

The administration of this site assumes no responsibility for anyone taking any actions because of information and/or materials contained within the Time Travel Institute site.

You alone are responsible for the content of your messages and the consequences of any such message. The Time Travel Institute Administration reserves the right to delete any message with or without cause or warning.

And remember, even if someone encourages you to post inappropriate content, you will still be held accountable. Do not let other users trick you into breaking the rules.

On Topic Discussion

If you want to start a thread, make sure to choose the most relevant forum section for your topic. Not only will posting in the wrong section make your topic harder to find, but it may cause your thread to be moved or removed. If you are replying to a thread, make sure your reply contributes to the topic. Off topic posts not only ruin a good thread but are grounds for post removal.

Please note that the Off-Topic section is available to discuss topics that are not directly related to the other sections in the Time Travel Institute Forum. The section cannot be used for spam, abusing the post count or rating system, or doing anything that would otherwise violate the rules or Terms of Service.

Spam

Spamming is not tolerated in the forum. Spamming includes posting the same post multiple times in the forum, repeatedly posting on the same topic in different threads, posting the same post in multiple sections, or posting repeatedly without actually contributing to a topic.

Multiple posts, whether in the same section or different sections, action will be taken as spam.

Thread Bumping

Posting messages with irrelevant content for the sole purpose of keeping certain posts near the top is called "bumping". Thread bumping is prohibited in the forum. It causes other posts to scroll off the page faster than they should, and raises forum traffic unnecessarily. Threads will be bumped up naturally if users have reason to contribute to them. If you have a question or issue that is not being addressed, make sure you contact a moderator to assist you.

Personal Information

Personal information is information that can be used to identify a person or contact him or her either on or off-line. It is a violation of our rules to post someone else's personal information. Furthermore, use caution when posting your own personal information. If someone requests your personal information (including contact information), consider that the person may not be who you think they are and may not have your best interests in mind. It's important to use good judgment and discretion to ensure all participants' privacy is protected.

Any actions taken by members off-line are also the sole responsibility of the individual members, the administration assumes no responsibility if any members reveal personal information to other members.

There is a privacy policy link at the bottom of the Forum page. For questions regarding the privacy policy of Time Travel Institute, use the link.

Advertising

The purpose of the forum is for discussion. As such, it should not be used to advertise, promote, or drive traffic to another site or notify users of a promotion or contest not approved by the Time Travel Institute staff. If you have relevant news or fan site link that contributes to a discussion, this will not likely be seen as advertising. If you are in doubt, ask a moderator.

Post Language

Our forum currently only supports English-language posts. Posts in other languages may be removed.

Inappropriate Posts/Threads

If you see a post that you think is inappropriate or possibly in violation of the Terms of Service, make sure to contact the Moderators. This will bring the post to the attention of the Administration who can remove inappropriate posts and action accounts as necessary to keep our community safe and productive for everyone.

Copyrighted Material

The use of copyrighted materials should be done so with express permission from the copyright holder. If it is brought to the attention of the Administration of Time Travel Institute that any violations of the copyright laws have occurred, the posts containing any such material will be deleted.

Multiple Accounts

Multiple accounts cannot be used to force thread activity or otherwise abuse any of the forum systems. If a second account is used to get around an account action, all related accounts may be removed . If you have an inquiry about a suspension or ban, make sure to contact the Moderators or the MOP to assist you. The Administration team will not give you account information about an account that does not belong to you.

Posting through a proxy will get a user banned and the IP address blocked at the server level.

Changing a Name or Moving Forum Stats

It is not possible for the Administration to change your screen name, post count, or rating.

Disputes and Debates

Show respect to others. Use constructive criticism, rather than being abusive.

If both sides have comparable evidence, split the topic and describe both sides.

Staff can intervene to enforce the above rules. If the staff decisions are not adhered to, posting privileges can be revoked with or without notice.

If you feel you've been unfairly banned or targeted, then you may appeal to the MOP directly. The MOP's e-mail address is webmaster at this domain.

Thank-you,

Time Travel Administration
