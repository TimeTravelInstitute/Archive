Alright, everyone, consider this:

This business of John Titor's death doesn't make any sense at all. Think about it. In the model of the universe that Titor unveiled in the original posts, time is revealed to be a gestalt entity. 1999, 2004, and 2036 are all three dimensional cross sections of the same four dimensional whole. There are as many "John Titors" within the life of John Titor as there are quanta of time within that span. (Quantized time is a so-far unproven and difficult to grasp concept, but that's not really the point.) The important fact here is that the gestalt John Titor persists despite his death in whatever year he supposedly dies in. It doesn't matter that he dies. The death is like the physical boundary of a three dimensional object. Just because you come to the end of a dead-end street doesn't mean the street ceases to exist. Anyway, any quantum of John Titor that coexists with and has access to a time machine could go back in time. The John who supposedly visited our time was just one of many who might have.
Also, why May of 2004? Why tell us now, out of all possible moments that "01" could have chosen to let us know about Titor's death? Our passage of time is not correlated with the passage of time in the 2030's. Get it? The John Titor of that worldline always dies. That event doesn't happen until it happens, and there's no reason someone witnessing it should decide to come back and tell anyone in the past. More to the point, and even greater gestalt John Titor, the John Titor made up of all the John Titors in all the parallel worldlines, continues living. Death of one is irrelevant. The more I think about this the more ridiculous it seems, and also the less able I am to make my point sufficiently articulate. I'll stop now, but I hope everyone can see the logic in what I'm trying to say. There is no sense in mourning John Titor's death. If he is who and what he says he is, he is currently a six year old with no knowledge of time travel.
