After an absence of two weeks, she finally posted again on December 2nd in the thread 'You Have Your new Pope', hinting 'It should also be noted that each Posting may be considered a jumble'. After that subtle push in the right direction, we slowly began figuring out that the poem was encoded in anagrams. A mere fifteen minutes after her first post that day, she posted again in the same thread, adding also that 'As I had previously stated, and Recently Supplied, Each of These Events will Come to Pass', and then reposting the same poem again.

Now, it seems to me that if we combine her three comments about this poem, we get : 'Each Posting may be considered a jumble [...] Each of These Events will Come to Pass [...] 2007-2009.'

It does not seem unreasonable to conclude from the above construction that each line is an anagram of a predicted event occurring sometime between 2007 - 2009. If so, then the proposed resolutions (DISASTERS LOOM : SHARIA LAW, AMERICA'S LAST PENNIES, MIDDLE EAST WAR, ECONOMIC COLLAPSE, MAHMOUD AHMADINEJAD, EARTHQUAKES RUMBLE, NUCLEAR ISLAM, SILKWORM STRIKE FLIES, ????? , IRAN'S SEA BLEEDS OIL, RARE ECOSYSTEM OFFENSE, MOTHER EARTH SIGHS) should all occur sometime over the next three years, if Zeshua is to be believed.

Furthermore, it seems we might go even farther and assume that the first line of the poem, 'disasters loom', provides an additional qualification to each of the subsequent lines, giving us another aid in trying to understand or interpret them. In other words, each of the lines of the poem not only describes an event predicted to come to pass over the next three years, but an event worthy of being called a 'disaster' ! Thus, the poem suggests that 'Sharia Law' not only exists in these years, but somehow becomes both a notable 'event' and a 'disaster' in those years, beyond what it is already. Additionally, one would thus also assume that the lines 'Middle East War' and 'Mahmoud Ahmadinejad' refer to new events and circumstances beyond what exist along those lines already today. Most of the other lines' predictions seem obvious, although line ten is still unsolved, and line 13 is rather obscure. I would interpret these lines as saying that either a whole new Middle East War breaks out in the next three years, or the ones that currently exist there will get a lot worse. Mahmoud Ahmadinejad is categorized as a disaster, which suggests that he will not turn away from his current path towards war. The poem includes the item 'earthquakes rumble' as one of the disasters, which raises some questions. Disastrous earthquakes are not all that uncommon, especially within a three-year window, which makes one wonder if the prediction is suggesting that the quantity and/or intensity of earthquakes will be significantly worse than normal over the next three years.
